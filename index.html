On-campus Parking Locations:

Farley Field House and Watson Arena

Open to daytime visitors and student parking. Largest lot on campus, can be accessed by taking a right onto Watson Drive. Close access to athletic fields, Greason pool, tennis courts, Watson arena, and Harpwell apartments.

Coffin Street Parking Lot

Open to daytime visitors and student parking. Central location with close access to Smith Union and Ladd house.

Admissions Lot, Burton-Little House

Open to admissions visitors and located off of Tower Drive. Central location near Hawthorne Longfellow library and Thorne hall.
